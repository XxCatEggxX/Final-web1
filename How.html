What is AI art?

How is it made?

  • AI art, the full name of artificial intelligence art, is art generated by computers rather than humans. A human has to give the generator a hint to create the artwork, but the computer does the actual conception, composition and creation. AI art is a new and exciting field of digital art that uses artificial intelligence to create new works of art. AI artwork can be developed in a variety of ways, but the most common are using algorithms to create images or using neural networks to generate new ideas. Some of the most famous AI artworks are generated by Google's DeepDream algorithm, which creates surreal and dreamlike images from real-world photographs. Other prominent examples of AI art include generative adversarial networks (GANs), which can be used to create realistic images of people or objects that don't exist. Regardless of the approach, AI art offers a fascinating insight into the potential of artificial intelligence to create new and beautiful things.
