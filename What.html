What is AI art?

How is it made?

  • Ultimately, AI brings to art what it brings to everything else it touches: the ability to extend the human mind to new levels of expression and understanding. It does this in much the same way as previous technologies: by simplifying the creative process and bringing clarity to the ideas expressed and even the manner in which they are expressed—whether in the high art world or in an advertising campaign. AI systems will also allow artists to experiment with new forms of creativity. For example, an artist can use an AI algorithm to generate an image to use as a starting point for a painting. This will allow them to explore new possibilities and create original work.
